To prevent participants from memorizing the novel graphemes based on resemblance to known graphemes we controlled for similarity to characters of extant writing systems by comparing each invented grapheme against the database of 11,817 characters (excluding Chinese, Korean, and Japanese) on the Shapecatcher website (Milde, 2011).
